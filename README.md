# Marblecross

Marblecross — Privacy Policy Last updated: February 2026

Marblecross does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data from its users. The app does not require account creation, does not connect to the internet, and does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking of any kind. All game data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted externally.
