Marblecross — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Local play: Marblecross does not require account creation and does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or any third-party tracking. All local game data is stored on your device.

Online multiplayer: When you use the online multiplayer feature, the app connects to Marblecross servers at marblecross.com. A randomly generated device identifier (not linked to your identity) and game activity data (moves, game results, difficulty settings) are transmitted to and stored on Marblecross servers periodically during play and at game completion, for gameplay and AI improvement. No personally identifiable information is collected. You can play in local mode at any time without any data leaving your device. To request deletion of your data, contact privacy@marblecross.com.

Data storage: Game data is stored on servers operated by Marble Mayhem LLC. Data is not sold or shared with third parties.

Contact: privacy@marblecross.com
